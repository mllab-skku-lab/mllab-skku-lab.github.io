Introduction












I am Principal Investigator (PI) in machine Learning Laboratory at Sungkyunkwan University, South Korea. I am expected to graduate with bachelor’s degree of Software in June 2020.

I am supported by Samsung Fellowship. Before joining this lab, I am researcher of ID Square Lab and Big Data Lab, both are belonging to College of Software, Sungkyunkwan University.

My general research area is applied machine learning for large interconnected systems. I focus on design optimal recommendation system on rich-labeled relational structures, such as knowledge graph. Also, I’m interested in various fields of mathematics, such as tensor factorization and differential geometry. I had experience with developing several personal projects, including recommendation system in Social Network Service, sports game simulator and AI art generator using various machine learning techniques.
